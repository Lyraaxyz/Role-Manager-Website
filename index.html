Powerful Features

Complete role management solution with configurable limits, bot inclusion options, and automatic cleanup systems

Auto Roles

Automatically assign up to 10 roles to new members when they join your server. Configure separate settings for bots and users with flexible permission management.

Reaction Roles

Set up to 20 reaction roles per server with 4 different modes: Toggle (add/remove), Add Only, Remove Only, and Reverse. Supports both standard and custom emojis.

Activity Roles

Assign roles based on 6 activity types: Playing, Streaming, Listening, Watching, Custom status, and Competing. Up to 10 activity roles with real-time presence detection.

Sticky Roles

Configure up to 10 sticky roles that automatically re-apply to users who had them before leaving. User data is securely stored for exactly 14 days then automatically cleaned up.

Easy Management

Powerful slash commands with autocomplete functionality. List all configurations, remove specific settings, or reset everything. Includes help command and system diagnostics.

Reliable & Secure

Enterprise-grade security and reliability for your Discord server role management needs.
